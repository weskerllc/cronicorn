## üé¨ Production Quick Reference

### Recording
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 @ 30fps
- **Software**: OBS Studio, Loom, or ScreenFlow
- **Browser**: Clean profile, no extensions, 100-125% zoom
- **Segments**: Record in pieces, edit together (easier than one take)

### Voiceover
- **Tone**: Professional but friendly, not sales-y
- **Pacing**: Slow down for key concepts, speed up for navigation
- **Equipment**: USB microphone (Blue Yeti, Rode NT-USB)
- **Script**: See full plan for complete voiceover script

### Editing
- **Software**: Premiere Pro, Final Cut, or DaVinci Resolve
- **Transitions**: Subtle crossfades (0.5s), avoid flashy effects
- **Music**: Optional, very subtle background (-25 to -30 dB)
- **Text**: Large, readable at 720p, high contrast

### Export
- **Master**: MP4, H.264, 1080p, 10-15 Mbps
- **Web**: MP4, H.264, 1080p, 5-8 Mbps
- **Mobile**: MP4, H.264, 720p, 2-4 Mbps
- **Preview GIF**: 800x600, 15fps, 10 seconds (AI adaptation moment)
